# Product Roadmap: AI Closet Organizer

**Timeframe:** 18 Months  
**Last Updated:** February 2025

---

## Vision
By Q4 2026, be the #1 closet organization app with 1M+ users wearing 40%+ of their wardrobe (vs. 20% industry average).

---

## Q2 2025: MVP Launch

### Must-Have
- AI photo cataloging (category, color, fabric auto-tagging)
- Daily outfit suggestions (3-5 outfits)
- Weather integration
- Outfit save & tracking
- Freemium model ($4.99/month premium)

### Success Criteria
- 10K downloads
- 60% onboarding completion
- 45% D7 retention

---

## Q3 2025: Iteration & Growth

### Features
- Calendar integration (event-appropriate outfits)
- "Hidden Gems" feature (underused item suggestions)
- Packing list generator (for travel)
- Referral program (give 1 month, get 1 month free)

### Goals
- 45K users
- 8% premium conversion
- Improve AI accuracy to 92%

---

## Q4 2025: Retention & Monetization

### Features
- Outfit history calendar view
- Style evolution insights ("Your style this year")
- Wardrobe utilization dashboard
- Shopping suggestions (items to complete outfits)

### Goals
- 100K users
- 8K premium subscribers
- 50% D30 retention

---

## Q1 2026: Platform Expansion

### Features
- Android launch
- Men's wardrobe support
- Social sharing (optional outfit posts)
- Integration with resale apps (Poshmark, Depop)

### Goals
- 250K users (40% Android)
- 20K premium subscribers
- Expand to UK, Canada
