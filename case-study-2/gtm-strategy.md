# Go-to-Market Strategy: AI Closet Organizer

**Launch Date:** Q2 2025  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Beta (Weeks 1-6)
- 500 TestFlight users
- Focus: AI accuracy, onboarding flow, outfit relevance
- Target: 60% onboarding completion, 45% D7 retention

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (Week 7)
- App Store submission (US only)
- PR outreach to fashion/tech media
- Influencer seeding (10 micro-influencers, 50K-200K followers)

### Phase 3: Full Launch (Week 10)
- Paid acquisition ($15K/month budget)
- App Store featuring push
- Target: 10K downloads month 1

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Professional women, 28-42, urban, $75K+ income, fashion-conscious, owns 60-100 items

**Secondary:** Eco-conscious millennials/Gen Z, 22-35, sustainability-focused, smaller wardrobes

**Channels:** Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, fashion blogs

---

## Positioning

**Tagline:** "Your closet, organized in 10 minutes. Your outfits, styled by AI."

**Key Messages:**
1. Setup in 10 minutes (vs. hours with competitors)
2. Never feel like you have "nothing to wear" again
3. Wear more of what you own, buy less

**Differentiation:**
- AI-powered = effortless
- Weather + calendar = contextual
- Sustainability-focused = values-aligned

---

## Channel Strategy

### Owned
- **App Store:** Optimized screenshots, keywords ("closet organizer," "outfit planner")
- **Landing page:** doordashcloset.com (capture emails pre-launch)
- **Blog:** Styling tips, capsule wardrobe guides (SEO)

### Paid
- **Instagram/Facebook:** Carousel ads showing before/after closet organization
- **TikTok:** Short-form videos of AI cataloging in action
- **Google Ads:** Keywords: "closet app," "outfit planner"
- **Budget:** $15K/month, CAC target: $18

### Earned
- **PR:** Pitch to TechCrunch, Vogue, Refinery29
- **Influencers:** 20 partnerships (mix of fashion + sustainability creators)
- **App Store featuring:** Submit for "App of the Day"

---

## Pricing & Monetization

**Free Tier:** 50 items, basic suggestions, ads  
**Premium:** $4.99/month or $39.99/year (20% discount)

**Launch Offer:** 30-day free trial of Premium
