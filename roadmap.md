## Vision & Strategy

### North Star
By Q4 2026, DoorDash will be the #1 food delivery platform for health-conscious users, with 50% of our active user base regularly engaging with nutrition features and a thriving premium health subscription tier generating $40M+ ARR.

### Strategic Themes
1. **Foundation** (Q2-Q3 2025): Launch core nutrition visibility and filtering
2. **Intelligence** (Q4 2025 - Q1 2026): Add personalization, ML recommendations, and integrations
3. **Monetization** (Q2-Q4 2026): Launch premium tier and B2B wellness programs

## Feature Prioritization Framework

### How We Decide What to Build

**Scoring criteria (1-5 scale, weighted):**
1. **User impact (30%):** How many users benefit? How much do they benefit?
2. **Business impact (30%):** Revenue, retention, or strategic value?
3. **Feasibility (20%):** Engineering effort, data availability, risk?
4. **Strategic fit (20%):** Aligns with long-term vision?

**Minimum score to prioritize:** 3.5/5.0

## Out of Scope (Intentionally Excluded)

The following features have been considered and intentionally excluded:

### Medical/Clinical Features
- Blood sugar tracking integrations (liability concerns)
- Prescription diet plan integration (requires medical licensing)
- Disease-specific meal plans (HIPAA, regulatory complexity)

**Rationale:** Avoid positioning as a medical tool; we're a food delivery platform with nutrition data.

### Gamification
- Points/badges for hitting macro goals
- Leaderboards, challenges, competitions

**Rationale:** Risk of encouraging unhealthy behaviors (restriction, comparison). Focus on information, not gamified motivation.

### Meal Kit / Ingredient Delivery
- Sell ingredients for users to cook
- Compete with Instacart/grocery delivery

**Rationale:** Separate business model, different operations. Partner rather than build.

### In-App Community Features
- Forums, user-to-user meal advice
- Social feed of meals

**Rationale:** Moderation complexity, low strategic value. Users have Reddit/Instagram for this.

## Appendix: Feature Backlog (Not Committed)

These are ideas under consideration but not yet scheduled:

### High Potential (Likely Future Inclusion)
- **Micronutrient tracking** (vitamins, minerals) - Data availability improving
- **Allergen warnings** - High safety value, moderate complexity
- **Voice ordering with nutrition constraints** - "Order me high-protein lunch under 600 cal"
- **Carbon footprint tracking** - Sustainability trend, differentiation opportunity

### Medium Potential (Needs Validation)
- **Meal photo nutrition estimation** - User uploads photo, we estimate macros
- **Group order macro summaries** - Show nutrition for entire group order
- **Nutrition "rewind"** - Suggest tomorrow's meals to balance yesterday's intake

### Low Potential (Parked for Now)
- **DNA-based meal recommendations** - Niche, accuracy unproven
- **Fasting timers** - Not core to delivery use case
- **Macro "banking"** - Save unused macros for later (encourages unhealthy restriction)
